Our Story

Born from African
Elegance & Pride

House of Queen Bee celebrates the richness of African textile traditions. Based at Rhodesville Mall in Harare, we design and craft each piece with intention — blending vibrant Ankara prints, luxurious Boubou silhouettes, and contemporary cuts that speak to the modern African woman.

From Zimbabwe Fashion Week 2025 to doorsteps around the world, our pieces carry the spirit of Africa wherever they go.
